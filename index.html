How It Works

Simple, intuitive UI
Perfect for any age

Multi-mode layout allows users to adjust the simplicity of their network. Even users with no tech experience can begin at a level that's comfortable for them.

Coupon-based
Incentive system

Enjoy earning discounts while you socialize with others! Learn how to maintain healthy social interaction by earning the rewards that you love.

Great way to interact with
Family and Friends

Social media integration allows you to easily communicate with your grandkids, friends, and other family members, even if they use other social networks. The built-in calendar allows you to keep track of local events and reminders.
